Learning Outcomes
Upon successful completion of this module, the student will be able to:

  1. Employ a model to assess the business environment.
  2. Discuss key issues in business social responsibility.
  3. Analyze business-related ethical considerations.
  4. Identify an industry for use in creating a future business scenario.

Introduction
This course has been designed to provide you with an introductory "big picture" view of business. The focus is on the forces acting outside the organization that influence business decisions. Additionally, the course exposes you to the initial stages of strategic planning. In the program's final course, MBA 741: Strategic Management, you will focus primarily on internal strategic analysis.
